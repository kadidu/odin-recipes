PANCAKE RECIPE

LEVEL easy

Ingredients:

  • a cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3 easpoons of baking powder
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoom vanilla extract
  • Maple syrup or chocolate syrup

Preparetions:

  1. Pour all the dry ingredients into a big bowl and mix
  2. Pour the egg,oil,milk and the vanilla into a medium bowl and mix
  3. Mix the dry ingredients and the medium bowl togther untill you get uniform texture
  4. Oil the pan and pour a small amount. when bubbles form, turn the pancake over to its other side. when it seems to be ready. put it on your plate
  5. Served with syrup of your choice
