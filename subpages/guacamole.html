GUACAMOLE RECIPE

LEVEL:very easy

Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tomato
  • A few cliantro/parsley leaves
  • 1 lemon juice>
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Preparetions:

  1. Cut the avocado and the tomato to little pieces and pour it into your bowl
  2. Tear the leaves of the cliantro/parsley into the bowl
  3. Add the rest and mix everything together
