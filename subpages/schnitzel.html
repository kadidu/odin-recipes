SCHNITZEL RECIPE

LEVEL:easy

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • 2 eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and papper
  • Oil
  • Ketchup sauce/barbecue sauce

Preparetions:

  1. Cut the chicken breast into thin and large slices
  2. Pour the eggs and mix it in a bowl. also make a plate with breadcrumbs, salt and papper
  3. Soak the chicken bbreast in the bowl of eggs, then place it on the plate with the salt, papper and breadcrums, let them stich the the chicken breast
  4. Heat oil in a high pan, and then fry the schnitzel
  5. When yo finish frting, place the schnitzel on absorbent paper and let t cool a little
  6. Serve the schnitzel with a sause of your choice
